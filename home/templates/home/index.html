About Us

Speakeasy Cocktails is a home delivery service for premium cocktails and personal bar equipment.

We provide a wide range of alcoholic and non alcoholic beverages, pre-prepared and ready to drink, all you need to do is add ice to your favourite glass and enjoy.

We deliver nationwide in Ireland. Delivery is free for all orders over €30 and charged at 10% of your order below €30.
